# 🧋 Pearl Pop

**Pop pearls. Stay in the flow.**

Pearl Pop is a casual arcade game inspired by classic brick breaker gameplay — reimagined with a boba (milk tea) aesthetic and satisfying, modern mobile feel.

Built by **CODEWERX LLC**

---

## 🎮 Overview

Pearl Pop is designed to be:
- simple to learn
- satisfying to play
- visually clean and aesthetic
- fast and replayable

Players control a **straw paddle** to bounce a **boba pearl** and clear all blocks in each level.

The focus is not complexity — it’s **feel, polish, and satisfying gameplay**.

## 🔥 Key Features (MVP)

- Classic brick breaker gameplay
- 20 handcrafted levels
- 4 themed worlds:
  - Brown Sugar
  - Matcha
  - Taro
  - Thai Tea
- Simple power-ups:
  - Multi-ball
  - Wide paddle
  - Sticky paddle
  - Slow motion
- Smooth physics + satisfying hit feedback
- Fast retry loop (no friction)

---

## ✨ Design Philosophy

Pearl Pop is built around:

### 1. Satisfaction First
Every hit should feel good:
- soft pop sounds
- particles
- smooth motion

### 2. Simple Gameplay
No unnecessary systems:
- no accounts
- no backend
- no complex menus

### 3. Fast Sessions
- quick start
- instant retry
- short levels

### 4. Visual Identity
- clean, modern aesthetic
- boba-inspired themes
- highly screenshot-friendly
